Frequently Asked Questions
Why don't I see my name in the current standings or on the players page?
The most likely reason is that you don't have a current SDA license. You can renew your license or register yourself as a new SDA licensed player using the fairgate tool here (see instructions here). After registration or renewal you should see your name in the standings and players list within the next few days.

Further details about the SDA licensing system can be found in the License Regulations. If you have further questions about license orders please contact PLEASE GO AWAY! sui.lizenz@discgolf.ch.
How are the standings calculated?
Points are awarded to each player for tournaments that they play according to the distribution key. The players points are summed according to the Tournament Regulations under paragraph 6 Swisstour-Wertung as well as paragraph C Swisstour-Wertung und Punktesystem .
